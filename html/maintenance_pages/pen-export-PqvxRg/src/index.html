Maintenance Information

Notice of System Maintenance

Domo will be performing system maintenance on your production instance Friday, August 21st 2015 starting at 7:00 pm PDT and ending on Sunday, August 23rd 2015 12:00 pm PDT. During this time access to the product will be intermittent while we apply necessary changes to our hosting environment.

To facilitate the ongoing administration and maintenance of Domo, we will be consolidating the IP addresses that you need to whitelist from 40 down to 4. Today, these 4 should already be in your list of 40.

What to Expect During this Time

Access to the system will be blocked until the planned maintenance is completed. Any user trying to access the system will not be able to login and instead will be presented with our maintenance page. Once the maintenance is complete all data uploads and processing will resume. Any jobs that were skipped due to this maintenance will be processed at the next available scheduled time.

What You Need to Do

If you have enabled IP Whitelisting for any of your data connections please make sure that each of the four IP Addresses below are included in that list.
